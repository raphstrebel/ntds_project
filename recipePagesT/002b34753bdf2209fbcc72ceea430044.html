Maple Oatmeal with Dried Fruit Recipe
  • Prep/Total Time: 15 min.
  • Yield: 4 Servings
    •
5 10 15

Ingredients

  • 3 cups milk or water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1-1/2 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 1/3 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/3 cup golden raisins
  • 3 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Directions

  • In a large saucepan, bring milk and salt to a boil. Stir in oats; cook for 1-2 minutes or until thickened, stirring occasionally. Remove from the heat. Stir in the cranberries, raisins, syrup and cinnamon. Yield: 4 servings.


Originally published as Maple Oatmeal with Dried Fruit in Simple & Delicious January/February 2008, p25
