28 Tasty Skewer Recipes for the Grill

Whether you call it a skewer, kabob, kebab, spedini, or saté, these portable and versatile meals-on-a-stick are perfect for outdoor grilling and barbecues. We've collected 28 skewer recipes with classic and international flavors — pair these skewers with a few grilled side dish recipes.

By Kara Rodemer

Scallop and Cherry Tomato Skewers

scallops, skewers, kebabs, grill, barbecue

Light, fresh, and easy-to-make, these grilled scallop and cherry tomato skewers get their spicy, tart flavor from lemon and Dijon mustard. Serve with our Grilled Caesar Salad.


Recipe: Scallop and Cherry Tomato Skewers

Middle Eastern Lamb Skewers

marinades, lamb recipes, skewers

Combine onion, garlic, parsley, lemon, allspice, and saffron in a marinade that's great on chicken breast as well as lamb.


Recipe: Middle Eastern Lamb Skewers

Thai Chicken Saté

skewers, kebabs, grill, barbecue, grilled chicken

For an Asian twist on barbecue, marinate tender slices of skewered chicken in a curried-coconut-milk blend and serve with a peanut dipping sauce. Pickled cucumbers make a perfect partner.


Recipe: Thai Chicken Saté

Yogurt-Marinated Lamb Kebabs with Lemon Butter

marinated lamb, skewers, kebabs, grill, barbecue

Taken from the Rajasthani countryside of India, this recipe calls for lamb marinated in yogurt to make it exceptionally tender. Then a basting of lemon butter, just before serving, gives it an extra burst of citrus flavor.


Recipe: Yogurt-Marinated Lamb Kebabs with Lemon Butter

Jambalaya Sausage Kebabs

sausage, skewers, kebabs, grill, barbecue

Bringing a well-loved Louisiana dish to the grill, these skewers are threaded with zucchini, red pepper, onion, and sausage coated with Cajun seasoning, then served with a side of rice.


Recipe: Jambalaya Sausage Kebabs

Cilantro-Lime Shrimp and Plum Kebabs

skewers, kebabs, grill, barbecue, grilled seafood Toss quick-cooking shrimp, juicy summertime plums, and zesty jalapeños with a simple cilantro-lime marinade for a deluxe meal in minutes. If you like, use peaches or nectarines in place of the plums and red or green bell peppers for the jalapeño.

Recipe: Cilantro-Lime Shrimp and Plum Kebabs

Grilled Rosemary-Salmon Spedini

salmon, skewers, kebabs, grill, barbecue

These Italian salmon kebabs are tossed with a lemony garlic and rosemary marinade before threading with fresh cherry tomatoes. If you can find them, use sturdy rosemary branches, stripped of leaves, as skewers. Oil your grill well to prevent sticking, don't move the kebabs around unnecessarily, and keep a close eye on the fire to avoid flare-ups. Serve with a fresh salad and brown rice for a full meal.


Recipe: Grilled Rosemary-Salmon Spedini

Sardinian Lamb Kabobs Over Couscous

lamb, skewers, kabobs, grill, barbecue

For a more formal summer get-together, these Sardinian skewers are perfect. Grill skewers of lemon-and-thyme-marinated lamb, and then serve them atop a garlicky Sardinian couscous full of cauliflower, currants, and pine nuts.


Recipe: Sardinian Lamb Kabobs Over Couscous

Grilled Buffalo Steak with Radicchio-Beet Skewers

buffalo, skewers, beets

Richly flavored buffalo steak is complemented by the bite of radicchio, mellowed a bit by the grill, the earthy-sweet beets, and a creamy goat cheese dressing. Buffalo is a tasty option but, because it's so lean, is best cooked rare to medium-rare.


Recipe: Grilled Buffalo Steak with Radicchio-Beet Skewers

Spanish Shrimp-and-Sausage Skewers

chorizo, shrimp recipes, skewers

To maximize the flavor quotient of these spicy Spanish skewers, try using authentic chorizo sausage in this recipe. Chorizo owes its characteristic flavor and color to mild Spanish paprika.


Recipe: Spanish Shrimp-and-Sausage Skewers

Turkey Kabobs with Garden Tomato Jam

grilling recipes, barbecue recipes, turkey recipes, kabobs Cut lean turkey breast into cubes, then marinate in a savory spice mixture. After grilling, serve with a quickly cooked combination of tomato and onion, sweetened with raisins and orange juice.

Recipe: Turkey Kabobs with Garden Tomato Jam

Vegetable Kabobs

vegetable kabobs recipe, gourmet grilling on a budget

This simple vegetarian side dish of grilled bell peppers and sweet onions tastes delicious with grilled meat or fish.


Recipe: Vegetable Kabobs

Scallops Yakitori

scallops, japanese recipes, cooking for two

"Yakitori" is a Japanese word that literally means "grilled bird," a reference to chicken bits on skewers in a sticky/salty sauce. The sauce itself is so favored that it has come to be known as "yakitori." Here we make yakitori with scallops, mushrooms, and scallions. Don't overcook the scallops; just a few minutes over the heat will do the trick.


Recipe: Scallops Yakitori

Spicy Lamb Shish Kebabs

lamb shish kebabs, kabobs, grilled meat recipe, skewers, leftover ideas

Marinated in thick Greek yogurt and a colorful mix of spices, these tender lamb chunks are tailor-made for a pita sandwich the next day.


Recipe: Spicy Lamb Shish Kebabs

Barbecue Beef Kebabs

tailgate recipes, grilling, kebabs, beef, corn Crisp Golden Delicious apples and musky cider vinegar add pizzazz to the sweet-and-savory coleslaw paired with these beef kebabs.

Recipe: Barbecue Beef Kebabs

Grilled Lamb Chops and Vegetable Kebabs

lamb chops, vegetable kebabs, grill recipes, couscous, easy dinner

Proof that fast dinners can also be gourmet, these lamb chops and vegetable skewers will be grilled to perfection in less than 15 minutes total.


Recipe: Grilled Lamb Chops and Vegetable Kebabs

Lemon-Garlic Lamb Kabobs

lamb kabobs, skewers, lemon, garlic, grill recipes, easy dinners

A tangy dipping sauce made with lemon juice, feta cheese, and salt and pepper goes well with these Greek-style skewers. Serve with herbed couscous or orzo pasta to round out the meal.


Recipe: Lemon-Garlic Lamb Kabobs

Thai Catfish Skewers with Savoy Cabbage Slaw

catfish, grilled seafood, skewers, cabbage slaw Give grilled fish a Thai twist with these three-step catfish skewers served atop a refreshing homemade slaw.

Recipe: Thai Catfish Skewers with Savoy Cabbage Slaw

Grilled Scallops

scallops, grilling recipes, skewers

Maya Kaimal's Mango-Chili glaze imparts a deep, caramelized flavor to these grilled scallops.


Recipe: Grilled Scallops

Salmon and Scallop Skewers with Romesco Sauce

cat cora, scallops, salmon, romesco sauce, skewers

This recipe for healthy skewers from Food Network's Iron Chef Cat Cora is great for grilling outdoors or baking in the oven when it gets colder.


Recipe: Salmon and Scallop Skewers with Romesco Sauce

Grilled Lamb with Salsa Verde

salsa verde, lamb recipes, skewers

Chunks of tender lamb grilled on skewers taste fantastic with a zesty salsa verde.


Recipe: Grilled Lamb with Salsa Verde

Jamie Oliver's Pork Kabobs

jamie oliver, pork recipes, kabobs

The key to making a good kabob is taking the time to ensure your ingredients are cut to roughly the same size and thickness. That way, everything cooks evenly and you get a lovely finished result.


Recipe: Jamie Oliver's Pork Kabobs

Spiced Kielbasa and Summer Fruit Kabobs

kielbasa, skewers, kabobs, grill, barbecue

Juicy apricots and plums are threaded with Polish sausage then coated with an orange marmalade and five-spice powder combination for an irresistible sweet and savory flavor mix.


Recipe: Spiced Kielbasa and Summer Fruit Kabobs

Grilled Beef-Tenderloin Skewers with Red-Miso Glaze

beef, miso glaze, skewers, kebabs, grill, barbecue

Addictively tangy and salty-sweet, these skewers are also delicious when made with chicken breast or pork tenderloin. The miso glaze is made with staple Asian condiments — soy sauce, red miso paste, and sesame oil — that you can easily find in the international foods aisle at your grocery store.


Recipe: Grilled Beef-Tenderloin Skewers with Red-Miso Glaze

Grilled Swordfish Kabobs

swordfish, grilling recipes, kabobs

Swordfish is an ideal fish for the grill. Its meatiness will hold up well over the heat.


Recipe: Grilled Swordfish Kabobs

Steak and Potato Kebabs with Creamy Cilantro Sauce

skewers, kebabs, grill, barbecue, healthy steak recipes

Steak kebabs get a Southwestern spin with poblano peppers and a creamy sauce spiked with cilantro, chile powder, cumin, and vinegar. The potatoes are partially cooked in a microwave before putting them on the grill to speed up their cooking time.


Recipe: Steak and Potato Kebabs with Creamy Cilantro Sauce

Indian Turkey Tikka Kebabs

indian recipes, kebabs, turkey recipes

The yogurt marinade in this Indian-inspired recipe infuses every tender morsel with the rich flavor of garam masala, a roasted spice blend that includes cumin, coriander, black pepper, and cardamom.


Recipe: Indian Turkey Tikka Kebabs

Herbed Scallop Kebabs

scallops, kebabs, grilling recipes

Grilled under a watchful eye to avoid overcooking, these skewers are a snap to prepare. A light lemon-and-herb marinade allows the sweet, succulent flavor of the scallops to shine. Be sure to purchase sea scallops, which are a good size for kebabs.


Recipe: Herbed Scallop Kebabs
