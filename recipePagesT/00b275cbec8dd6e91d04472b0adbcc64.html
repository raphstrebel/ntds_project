A Delicious Christmas Eve Buffet

Bon Appétit  | Karen Kaplan and Kristine Kidd

menu servings:30

print shopping list
Pulling together an open-house buffet for 30 is a snap with our no-panic, do-ahead menu.

  • Shrimp and Crab with Cocktail Salsa
    print
  • Caviar Dream with Endive and Baby Potatoes
    print
  • Platter of sliced Black Forest ham or Whole Honey-Glazed Ham and Havarti Cheese

    print
  • Fennel, Beet and Orange Salad with Olives

  • Brussels Sprout and Apple Salad with Blue Cheese and Walnuts

  • Champagne

    print
  • Fruitcake
  • Assorted cookies, brownies, and tartlets
This special buffet party for 30 is perfect for Christmas or New Year's Eve. If the words "buffet for 30" make you cringe, don't panic. We start with a purchased ham, and bread and cheese, and then surround them with a variety of dishes that are easy, sophisticated, and delicious. You can prepare most of each recipe in advance; the only things left to do on the day of the party are to add the finishing touches, set a pretty table, and put out the food. If you serve all the dishes, you'll have a most impressive spread, but you can offer as many or as few as you like. And if you are inviting fewer than 30 people, you can halve or quarter the recipes with equally splendid results.
