French Onion Soup

The classic French onion soup consists of caramelized, slow-cooked onions which are paired with a rich beef stock. Individual portions are topped with a large crouton and cheese and broiled until golden brown. French onion soup is always a favorite, including variations like vegetarian and baked versions.
