Pisco Sour recipe

rating
9.3
4.7

Tweet

Scan me to take me with you
serve in
alcohol
22%
options
3 parts pisco brandy
1 1/2 parts lemon juice
1 - 2 tbsp sugar

Add all ingredients to a mixer with ice. Shake well (until ice is melted), and serve in a cocktail glass.
