Appetizer Recipes

Great appetizers set the stage for a great meal. Of course, you can also enjoy them as a quick snack, too. Our collection of delicious appetizer recipes include hot and cold appetizers, spreads, dips, pinwheel roll-ups and chicken wings. Whether you're planning a large dinner party or just looking for a quick bite on the go, we have you covered.

Southwest Avocado Bean Dip

posted by: a cook | on: 12/22/2011
"I love this recipe! We made this last year at my friend's tailgate party. Only thing I made adjustment were using kosher salt...it made a big difference. I added a bit more sour cream and a bit more cumin. I also added garlic powder. It was superb! :)"
total time
30 min
servings
4 cups dip or 32 servings, 2 Tbsp. dip and 14 chips each

Barbecue-Bacon Party Spread

Cobb Salad Dip
