Favorite Pork Chop Recipes

Pork Chop Casseroles, Skillet Meals, and Slow Cooker Favorites

By Diana Rattray, About.com Guide

See More About:
  • pork chops
  • boneless pork chops
Here are some of my favorite pork chop recipes, along with some of the most popular and highly-rated recipes using pork chops. Choose from delicious casseroles, easy skillet meals, slow cooker chops, and more.

Pork Chop and Stuffing Casserole

Image © Diana Rattray
This easy pork chop casserole recipe is perfect for an everyday family dinner. Add a tossed salad for a quick and easy weekday meal.
Pork Chop and Stuffing Casserole

Pork Chops With Apples and Cranberries

Image © Diana Rattray
Chopped apples, cider, and dried cranberries jazz up these easy boneless pork chops. Serve these chops with sweet potatoes or rice, along with green beans, a tossed salad, or your favorite side vegetable.
Pork Chops With Apples and Cranberries

Cornbread Stuffed Pork Chops

Image © Diana Rattray
These are thick pork chops with a delicious cornbread stuffing mixture. This recipe can easily be cut down to make a special dinner for two.
Cornbread Stuffed Pork Chops

Apple Stuffed Pork Chops

Image © Diana Rattray
Big thick pork chops are stuffed with an apple and bread crumb mixture for a delicious family meal.
Apple Stuffed Pork Chops

Pork chops With Tangy Creole Mustard and Pineapple Sauce

Image © Diana Rattray
Use boneless or bone-in pork chops in this recipe for pork chops with a flavorful sauce. This dish can also be made in an electric skillet.
Pork chops With Tangy Creole Mustard and Pineapple Sauce

Pork Chops With Chutney Sauce

Image © Diana Rattray
Use an electric skillet to make these pork chops, or cook them on the stovetop. The sauce is an easy blend of chutney and seasonings. Serve these flavorful pork chops with rice or potatoes.
Pork Chops With Chutney Sauce

Pork Chops With Apple-Cranberry Chutney

Image © Diana Rattray
Pork Chops are glazed and pan grilled or broiled then served with a flavorful homemade apple cranberry chutney.
Pork Chops With Apple-Cranberry Chutney

Balsamic Glazed Pork Chops

Image © Diana Rattray
Balsamic vinegar and apricot preserves help to flavor these tasty skillet pork chops. Serve them with a tossed salad, rice, and biscuits or warm yeast rolls.
Balsamic Glazed Pork Chops

Cranberry Orange Pork Chops

Image © Diana Rattray
A delicious combination of pork with oranges and cranberries. Scroll down to see more pork chop recipes.
Cranberry Orange Pork Chops

Maple Glazed Pork Chops

Image © Diana Rattray
Maple syrup and spicy mustard flavor these flavorful skillet pork chops. Serve these tasty pork chops with sweet potatoes or mashed potatoes and peas or green beans.
Maple Glazed Pork Chops
